FAQ And Terms of service

What happens when I place an order?
Once we receive your order, we will wrap your order and have it delivered.
Or if you have chosen pickup, you will receive an e-mail to come and collect your order.



How long Will delivery take?

Depending on the order merchandise will take 1-3 days whilst parts
Can take 1-6 weeks.



Why is my order taking so long?

If your merchandise hasn't Arrived Within a week after we sent you that
your order has been shipped or sent with DHL please contact us
So that we can handle it. So that You don't have to worry!
As for parts These can take up to 2,5 Months. Our Parts are sometimes held up by customs.
We sadly cannot control that. However We are working on small distribution centres
around certain parts in the world so that we can solve this.

Do you guys wrap orders in gift wrapping

Yes simply say that you want Gift wrapping in the special note or request box when you order!



Why do I need to make an appointment?

Since we have our own projects we are very busy sometimes.
So in order to help you as a customer and help you more quickly
and resolve your issue and repair in the quickest way possible
you need to make a appointment. if you want to order a special edition
you don't need to make a appointment.



Return policy merchandise

after we have received your order back to us we wil happily refund your money



return policy parts, wraps and cosmetic car accessoires

what do you guys mean by lifetime warranty?

we are confident enough that the quality of our products can out live the customer. with this we mean that as long
